Some places that you can sleep in Tropoja

VITA Guesthouse

Situated in Bajram Curri in the Kukës County region, VITA Guesthouse has a garden. Boasting a shared kitchen, this property also provides guests with a picnic area. There is an outdoor fireplace and guests can make use of free WiFi and free private parking. At the guest house, every unit is fitted with bed linen and towels. Guests are welcome to relax in the on-site lounge, while packed lunches are also available upon request. The nearest airport is Pristina International Airport, 139 km from the guest house.

Royal Land

Royal Land in Theth offers adults-only lodging with garden, bar, and restaurant. Enjoy free parking and WiFi, plus outdoor amenities like a fireplace and picnic area with mountain views. Each unit includes a dressing room, private bathroom with shower, slippers, hair dryer, and toiletries. Bed linen and towels are provided. Packed lunches available for excursions. Families catered to with outdoor play equipment, baby safety gate, and babysitting. Explore cycling and hiking nearby. Theth National Park is 4.1 km away. Nearest airport is Podgorica, 86 km with paid shuttle service.

Hotel Lahuta

Set in Bajram Curri, 45 km from Visoki Dečani Monastery, Hotel Lahuta offers accommodation with a shared lounge, free private parking, a terrace and a bar. The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, a concierge service and currency exchange for guests. The units at the hotel come with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a safety deposit box and a private bathroom with a shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer. At Hotel Lahuta every room includes bed linen and towels. The nearest airport is Pristina International Airport, 122 km from the accommodation.
